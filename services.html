My services

For Organizations

Do you work with people from other countries and cultures?. If so, how you and your organization make sure the inclusion and integration are done properly?

Is your organization aware of the cultural bias?

What I can offer your organization is:

  • Cultural differences mapping. The mapping is done through employee assessment. The results will show you where and how the cultures differ from one another.
  • Organizational culture mapping. This mapping is related to how psychological safety is experienced and how this is interrelated to the culture in the organization.
  • Workshop. How to understand and standardized all cultures into the organizational culture.

For personal development and growth

If you are living in a different country than the one your grow up in or if you are curious and want to become more aware of the Culture you are living in, then this is for you

  • Personal cultural mapping. If you are from another culture, you will learn to read the culture you are in so that you can grow and thrive.
  • Workshop. Get tools to deal with people from different culture, either at work or on a personal level.
